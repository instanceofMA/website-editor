The Future of Web Design is Colorful

By Jane Doe â€¢ Dec 26, 2025

Web design has evolved significantly over the past decade. We've gone from skeuomorphic designs to flat design, and now we are entering an era of vibrant gradients and immersive experiences.

Colors evoke emotions. Using a strong primary gradient, like the one used in this demo (Sunset Orange to Sunshine Yellow), creates a feeling of warmth, energy, and optimism.

Why Gradients?

Gradients allow for a more natural feel than solid flat colors. They mimic the lighting we see in the real world. When paired with heavy drop shadows and rounded corners, they create a "Glassmorphism" or "Neomorphism" feel depending on the execution.
